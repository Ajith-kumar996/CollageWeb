History

Sri RangaPoopathi College of Arts, Science and Education was going established in the year 2016 under the aegis of Sri Rangapoopathi Educational and Charitable Trust. The College is duly approved by National Council for Teacher Education and Affiliated to Tamil Nadu Teacher Education University , Chennai. SRPCASE has it own Green campus spread over 40 acres in Alampoondi, located strategically in the Chennai to Thiruvannamalai State Highways. The campus is about 7 kms from Gingee, 35 kms from Tindivanam and 43 kms from Villupuram.
