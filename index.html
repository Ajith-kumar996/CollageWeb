The College runs more number of buses for providing transport facility to students from various places in the city this service is offered ensuring a hassle-free and safe transportation.

The College will provide separate hostel accommodation for both Boys and Girls.Students seeking hostel accommodation are required to give a written request countersigned by their parent guardian. At the time of admission to the college and hostel.

The objective of the Canteen and meal service is to protect by reducing the risk of foodborne illness, with proper sanitary conditions, and preventing adulterated foodthe College Canteen has beenfunctioning efficiently.
