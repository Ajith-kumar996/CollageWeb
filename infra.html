Infrastructure

Campus:

The Institution has all the physical Infrastructure as per National Council for Teacher Education norms. It is run on its own building with a permanent address. A three-storeyed building with the following facilities are available for the Staff and Learners:
-Adequate number of classrooms
-Staff room
-Principal’s room
-Administrative Block
-Library and Reading room
-ICT Resource Centre
-Store room
-Seminar Hall with PA system
-Laboratories (Biological Science, Computer Science, Psychology and Physical Science)
-Educational Technology room
-Resource centers
-Common room for female candidates
-Canteen
-Hygienic toilets – separately for male and female candidates
-Sports room
-Playground adjacent to the College building

Science & Mathematical Resource Center:

• Laboratory facilities are available for the following departments: Biological Science, Physical Science, and Psychology. All the Laboratories are updated and enhanced with the necessary equipments according to the periodical revision of the Tamilnadu Teachers Education University syllabus in science subjects and according to the requirements of the students. The maintenance of the equipments and other facilities in the laboratories are ensured by entrusting the responsibility with the respective staff of the science subjects.
